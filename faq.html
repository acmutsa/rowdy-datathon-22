F.A.Q.

What is a datathon?

A datathon is a weekend-long event where you can collaborate with other students on a data science project to learn new skills, create social impact, and innovate new solutions! There’s also plenty of time for networking with our partners, meeting other hackers, and attending workshops and mini events! In short, it’s a weekend dedicated to collaboration, data science, and community :)

Who can attend?

Any university or college student 18 or older can attend!

How much does it cost?

The Rowdy Datathon is completely FREE to attend, thanks to our awesome partners. You don't have to worry about a thing -- we'll provide meals, snacks, t-shirts, swag, prizes, and more all weekend long!

What should I bring?

The Rowdy Datathon is a weekend-long event, and we want to make sure you’re prepared! You are encouraged to bring a change of clothes, Any hardware you plan on using to complete your project, we will also be providing food but you're free to bring your own too!

What if I don’t know anything about coding or data science?

No worries! Our datathon is for everyone, regardless of experience! We will have plenty of mentors, resources, and workshops to help you learn throughout the weekend no matter what your skill level is.

How big can my team be?

Teams can consist of up to 4 hackers! While it isn’t required, we do encourage attendees to participate as part of a team. Don't have a team? No worries, we will have an opportunity before the event for you to meet other hackers and form teams!

How long is this hackathon?

This year our datathon will be a 36-hour event, 24 hours of which will be dedicated to hacking.

What if I’m not a UTSA student?

No problem! The Rowdy Datathon is open to ANY college, university, or vocational student over 18 years old. We're so excited to see students from all over collaborating and innovating together at this year's event!

What if this FAQ didn’t answer my question?

Please e-mail us at team@RowdyDatathon.org! We’ll be happy to answer any questions you have.
